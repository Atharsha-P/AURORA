About

Welcome to the Clinical Interviews platform. We provide free therapy sessions for individuals seeking mental health support. Our clinical interviews are conducted by experienced therapists who offer three free sessions to help you address your concerns.

During these sessions, our therapists will guide you through a structured interview process to understand your thoughts, feelings, and experiences. They will provide support, coping strategies, and resources tailored to your needs.

Don't forget to schedule your sessions and make the most out of this opportunity for self-care and growth. You will receive reminders for your upcoming sessions to ensure you don't miss them.
